My family

My parents

  • I don't have a large family but I'm really glad to tell you about them. I'd like to start it by telling about my father. My father is Francois Valentin Jacquit. He is from Toliara, in the south of Madagascar, and born there. He is a farmer. He is a confident and friendly father, that's why he always tells me to be as him. My mother's name is Rasoarinajaina Fanjarivelina and she is from Amparafaravola, a district in Alaotra Mangoro region. She is a teacher since 2008 until now and teaching in a small village called Maromandia at the moment, about 8km from Andilamena. It's a bit tiring for her to work in this place, because she has to walk for two hours and half to go to and from this place everyday. However, she is trying hard to do that even though she is getting old now. She is a good example for me. I'm really proud still having them. they are my strengh.
  • They both are living in Andilamena now, a little twon in the north of Alaotra Mangoro. We have been living in this place since 1992, when my father came fisrt there. I really love this place because it's surronded by green spaces. It has a lot of resources natural.

Stellah is my last sister, born in 2018, the same date as mine. That's all about my siblings.
