About ONJA.

I'm studying at ONJA school, which is a NGO lead by SAM and located in Mahanoro. We are twenty students at ONJA, also, this organisation is based on supporting the student who can't go to university to learn english and coding.We studyed english last up to now, but this year we focus more about coding.

However, at the beginning, I found it really hard to get started and to get use to it, but in fact practice pushed me to get over that.
