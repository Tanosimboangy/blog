About ONJA.

I'm studying at ONJA school, which is a NGO lead by SAM and located in Mahanoro. We are twenty students at ONJA, to learn english and coding with seven staff. This organisation is based on supporting the student who can't go to university but still want to study.We studyed english last year up to now, but this year we focus more on coding.
